About the Site

Nikoo Art is a curated selection of contemporary artworks — from abstract paintings to delicate illustrations. Each piece explores form, color, and emotion with a minimalist approach.

Commissions are available on request. If you have a concept in mind — a palette, a memory, or a space to transform — we can shape a bespoke piece together.

Feel free to reach out for collaboration, prints, or exhibition inquiries.
